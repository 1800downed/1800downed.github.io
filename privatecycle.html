About PrivateCycle

Your Privacy, Our Priority

PrivateCycle is a completely private, offline period tracking app. All your data stays on your device—no servers, no cloud, no tracking.

What We Don't Do

  • No account required
  • No internet connection needed
  • No analytics or ads
  • No third-party data sharing
  • No tracking or profiling

How We Keep You Safe

  • Optional PIN lock for your phone
  • Encrypted backups with AES-256
  • Open-source code—inspect it yourself
  • No authentication or login

How Predictions Work

PrivateCycle uses Naegele's rule and cycle pattern analysis for accurate predictions:

  1. Calculate average cycle length from your last 6-12 cycles
  2. Add average length to your last period start date
  3. For Trying to Conceive: estimate ovulation at day 14 before next period
  4. Show fertile window 5 days before to 1 day after ovulation
  5. Adjust predictions based on variations in your cycles

Medical Disclaimer
This app is for tracking and education only. It is not a contraceptive tool and should not be relied upon for preventing pregnancy. Consult a healthcare provider for medical advice.
