Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Raised for Today

Not Your Grandma's Cellar started with a simple conviction: the best preserves are made the old way — fresh produce, proper process, and patience. What Grandma perfected in her cellar, we've scaled into a small-batch operation serving Lincoln County and beyond.

Every jar is water-bath or pressure canned to USDA standards. Every tomato, berry, and bloom is sourced from our own farm or from neighbors we trust by name.
